a bit about us

Taylor & Scott met in 2009 while both were working as interns for PSI. Since their first interaction was over Skype (Taylor was living in DC and Scott was in Bangkok) they can technically say that they met online.

Work eventually brought Scott to DC where Taylor proceeded to flirt with him endlessly from her cubicle. Years of obliviousness, horrible accents and friendship blossomed into love after a day of paddleboarding and Mad Max watching in 2015.

At that point the real love story began as Scott fell head over heels for Dolly (originally Taylor’s dog, now their 11-lbs. Chihuahua mix).

Taylor loves that Scott is adventurous and pushes her to try new things even when she’s feeling trepidatious. Scott loves Taylor’s thoughtfulness, organization, and willingness to put up with his crazy and constant “teasing”. Both Taylor & Scott are very happy to be celebrating this special occasion with you.
